Sensible DateTime

Sensible DateTime is a jQuery plugin that makes it easy to format ISO datetime into something sensible as you see fit.

Intro

There are a handful of Javascript libraries that convert date on your page to relative time or format them in a kinda predefined way.

Sensible DateTime provides more flexibility according to your specific needs and context.
